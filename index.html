About Us

This site is created to support artists who may not have access to the resources or encouragement they need. We are dedicated to building a community that uplifts artists, helping them gain recognition and support from their peers and fans. Our mission is to cultivate a positive environment where artists can freely express themselves and receive the feedback they deserve.

Community Rules:

  • No abuse towards any artist.
  • If someone follows you, follow them back in the group.
  • Don't make fun of any artist or embarrass them.
  • Be a supportive and positive presence in the group.
  • No spam in the group chat.
